# Family feedback

**Loved:**
- Eco park Stamboliyski, it's an amazing park full of playgrounds for little kids
- Natural History Museum in Plovdiv, my daughter absolutely adores it
- Pazardjik Park in the island in the river, there's loads of things to do including small animals
- Stara Zagora Zoo is a lovely thing to do on a nice weather day

**Not interested in:**
- General walking in the Plovdiv Old Town, we live right in front and unless there is a good reason (eg an event), we are not interested in just walking around the old town or going to the Old Theater
- Aqualand Plovdiv — the water park permanently closed after the 2023 season and its infrastructure was dismantled.
- Asen's Fortress (Asenova Krepost), the Kuklen Waterfall, or the Belintash plateau as family outings — the ascents are steep, slippery and unrailed, unsafe for a 4-year-old. Prefer gentler alternatives (e.g. Momina Skala or Byala Reka ecotrails).

**Constraints:**
- None
